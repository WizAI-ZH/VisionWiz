## English

- Fixed the capture record modal so large photo sets stay inside the middle scroll area.
- The Open Folder and Close buttons now remain visible with safer spacing above the Windows taskbar and window edge.
- Capture thumbnails are slightly smaller, making dense photo records easier to browse.
- The same modal safe-area layout is applied to image classification and object detection training detail dialogs.
